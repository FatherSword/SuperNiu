About Us

SuperNiu has been dedicated to water meter production since 2010. Over the past 4 years, we have focused on the Southeast Asian market, especially Malaysia and Indonesia. Our three star - product water meters have occupied approximately 10% of the market share in this region. Last year alone, our sales volume reached 80,000 units, showing a steady upward trend.

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. Through continuous market research, we are proud to announce that over 90% of our customers are highly satisfied with our products. They praise our water meters for their high precision, durability, and easy installation. Our professional after - sales service team has also contributed to this excellent score, resolving customer issues in a timely manner.
